Terahertz spectroscopy can probe biomolecular rotational and vibrational
signatures, but robust classification of amino-acid fingerprint spectra remains
challenging under scattering noise and varying experimental conditions. This
work introduced a discrete Gaussian process and maximum likelihood model for
classifying 20 amino acids, achieving high accuracy with substantially reduced
training and inference cost.
