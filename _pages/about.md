I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate in mathematics at Qiuzhen College, Tsinghua
University, advised by Prof. Chenglong Bao. My current research focuses on
combining AlphaFold with cryo-EM observations for protein structure prediction.

More broadly, I am interested in AI for science, computational imaging, and
data-driven methods for molecular and biological systems. Before joining
Tsinghua University, I received my B.S. in Statistics from the School of
Mathematics, Sichuan University.

My recent work develops methods that connect deep protein-structure priors with
limited or imperfect experimental observations, aiming to improve atomic model
refinement when reconstructed maps are weak, anisotropic, or incomplete.

**Research interests:** AI for science, protein structure prediction,
computational imaging.
